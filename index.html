Paula Gardiner

I am Paula Gardiner (MSc). I am qualified to masters level in both CBT and neurospecialist physiotherapy. I am a CBT practitioner accredited with the BABCP and also a supervisor of other CBT therapists.

I also teach CBT at Masters level at the South of Scotland CBT training centre.

Treatment sessions can be remote, or from my clinic in the historic town of Bridge of Allan.

Specialities

  • Functional Neurological Disorders (www.neurosymptoms.org)
  • Anxiety disorders (including panic attacks, General Anxiety Disorder, Heath Anxiety Disorder, phobias)
  • Depression
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  • Post-traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)
  • Long term Neurological Conditions
